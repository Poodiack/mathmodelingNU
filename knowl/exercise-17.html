Exercise 1.

Suppose a biologist records the number of pulses per second of the chirps of a cricket at different temperatures (in \({}^\circ\)F). The data collected is shown below.

Temperature 72 73 89 75 93 85 79 97 86 91
Pulses/sec 16 16.2 21.2 16.5 20 18 16.75 19.25 18.25 18.5
(a)

Fit a straight line to this data (where temperature is on the \(x\)-axis). How well does the model fit the data?

(b)

What is the slope of this line? What does the sign of the slope tell you about the relationship between pulses/sec and temperature?

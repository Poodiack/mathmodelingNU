Simulate a cash register as follows. Suppose a small foodstand in a subway station sells sodas ($1.50 each), bananas ($0.75 each), sandwiches of various types (all $3.75 each), and bottles of water ($2 each). Write a subroutine that takes five inputs: the number of sodas, bananas, sandwiches and bottles of water, as well as the amount of cash tendered (for example, $20 or $10). Your program should tell the user how much change, in dollars and cents, is therefore due.

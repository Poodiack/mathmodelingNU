Exercise 4.

A cashier at a supermarket can checkout, on average, 3 customers a minute (this is called the service rate). Customers arrive, on average, 2 a minute (this is called the arrival rate). The manager figures that since the service rate is greater than the arrival rate, customers will never have to wait in line. What assumptions is the manager making? Do these assumptions seem reasonable? What does this say about the validity of the conclusion?

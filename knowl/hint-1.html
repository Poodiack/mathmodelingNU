Hint 6.2.2.1.

You should probably build your list of points piece-by-piece from the numbers given in the Population Growth example. From the initial population list, build a new list of the differences in the populations, build the list of products of the population counts \(p_n\) and \(621-p_n\) and, finally, build the list of points with first coordinate \(p_n(621-p_n)\) and second coordinate \(\Delta p_n\text{.}\)

The points command will plot a list of points.
